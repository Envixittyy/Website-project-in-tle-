Different Kinds of Colors

Primary colors

These are colors that cannot be created through the mixing of other colors
  1. Red
  2. Yellow
  3. Blue

Secondary colors

Colors made by mixing of two primary colors in a given color space
  1. Orange
  2. Purple
  3. Green

Tertiary colors

Combinations of primary and secondary colors
  1. Red-orange
  2. Yellow-orange
  3. Yellow-green
  4. Blue-green
  5. Blue-violet
  6. Red-violet
